I Love You! ❤️

I loved you
without a promise,
without a future,
without my name beside yours.

Not to compete,
not to steal,
not to beg the universe to rewrite your story —
but to love you quietly
from the corner where unchosen hearts sit.

I know I hurt you sometimes.
My impatience,
my fear of losing you,
my desperate hands trying to hold
what was never mine to hold.

And for that,
I carry regret like a shadow.

You may never walk toward me.
You may never look back.

You may build your forever
with someone braver, better, steadier.

And I —
I may fail a thousand times
trying to become worthy in your memory.

But one day,
even if I am just a passing thought,
I hope you remember me
not as the boy who wanted too much,

but as the one
who loved you
without conditions,
without ownership,
without expecting to win.

Even if I was never your choice,
I will grow.
I will rise.
I will become someone
who no longer loves from lack,
but from strength.

And maybe,
from somewhere far away,
you will hear of me
and smile —

knowing
that once,
someone loved you
so deeply
he chose to become better
instead of bitter.

Even if not yours.
Even if not mine.
